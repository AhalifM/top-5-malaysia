# Product Context

## Product Name
Swifty Agency

## Product Purpose
A bilingual (English / Malay) UGC content agency landing page for a Malaysian digital marketing company specialising in TikTok and Instagram Reels. The site must convert visitors into leads via WhatsApp CTAs while communicating affordability, credibility, and creative expertise.

## Register
brand

## Users
Malaysian business owners (SMEs in F&B, beauty, lifestyle, home) looking for affordable short-form video content. They are mobile-first, social-media-savvy, and price-conscious. They need to feel trust and excitement before clicking the WhatsApp CTA.

## Brand
- **Colors:** Champagne gold (#D4AF37) on near-black. Dark theme only.
- **Tone:** Confident, energetic, premium yet accessible. Never corporate.
- **Language:** Bilingual EN / MY with a toggle in the navbar.
- **Anti-references:** Generic SaaS hero metrics template. Neon-on-black crypto aesthetic.

## Strategic Principles
1. Gold is the accent — every CTA, highlight, and emphasis uses it. Black is the canvas.
2. Every section should feel like scrolling through a premium editorial magazine.
3. Motion is purposeful: entrance animations on scroll, micro-interactions on hover. No gratuitous spinning.
4. Admin panel at /admin — clean, functional dashboard to edit all content via URL/link inputs. No file uploads; everything is a link.
